## Standard nav order (all pages)
Home | Projects | Services | About | Sax Services | Contact | View Live Demos | More ▼ | Hire Me button

## More dropdown contents (in order)
1. Products (bi-box-seam icon, text-primary)
2. Testimonials (bi-chat-quote-fill icon, text-primary)
3. Certifications (bi-award-fill icon, text-primary)
4. — divider —
5. Sponsor Me (bi-heart-fill icon, text-danger)
6. Download CV (bi-file-earmark-pdf-fill icon, text-danger)

**Why:** Products was originally orphaned after the More dropdown on every page. It belongs inside More to keep the top nav clean and consistent.

**How to apply:** Any new page must include this nav structure verbatim. Active class goes on the matching top-level item (or on the dropdown-item inside More for Testimonials, Certifications, Sponsor Me, Download CV, Products).
